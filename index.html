I’m a 3rd year PhD student in Operations Management at Harvard University (Business School and the Graduate School of Arts & Sciences). My research interests are in Supply Chains, Retail Operations and Econometrics. I am grateful to be advised by Professor Ananth Raman.

I’m originally from Vancouver, Canada. Prior to Harvard, I obtained a B.A. in Economics from Simon Fraser University and an M.A. in Economics from Northwestern University.
